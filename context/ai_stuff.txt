OFF: ai is COMPLETELY off, always use fallbacks

LITE: ai is off, unless someone sends a custom message to them, in which case an ai reply can be generated

FULL: ai is on, so it should always send an ai generated message (this message should be written with knowledge about the current situation and what the message is supposed to say)
the only exception to this is if the ai is sending a message to another ai, which the player will most likely not read

ABSOLUTE: like full ai but the ai will send an ai message to other ai even though the player won't read it
